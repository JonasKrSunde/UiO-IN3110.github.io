Higher Level Programming 2020

A course taught at the University of Oslo


Latest version - 2020

This lecture series introduces concepts of higher level programming. The lecture introduces essential tools to quickly and efficiently implement programming problems.

The assignments are available on the University course website. The The images link to the lecture scripts. . You can also run the lecture slides interactively on your browser . The video recordings are available on this YouTube channel

The lecture was initially created by Hans Petter Langtangen and extended by Joakim Sundnes, Ola Skavhaug, Jonathan Feinberg, Karl-Erik Holter, Vidar Tonaas Fauske, Benjamin Ragan-Kelley, Lisa Pankewitz, Sebastian Mitusch and Simon Funke simon@simula.no. It is being taught on a yearly basis at University of Oslo under the name IN3110/IN4110.
